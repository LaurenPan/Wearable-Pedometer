Getting started

  1. Turn on Bluetooth in Settings on your iPhone to allow "nRF Utility" to connect to Bluetooth Smart peripherals
  2. Touch the icon of the required peripheral
    • HRM – connect to
      • heart rate monitor
    • Temp – connect to
      • health temperature sensor
    • Proximity – connect to
      • proximity sensor
    • Biking – connect to
      • heart rate monitor and/or
      • bike speed/cadence combo sensor
    • BP – connect to
      • blood pressure meter
    • BGM – connect to
      • blood glucose meter
  3. Activate your sensor and touch the connect button on the screen of your iPhone

Configure settings

Open settings by touching the cog in the upper right corner of your iPhone
