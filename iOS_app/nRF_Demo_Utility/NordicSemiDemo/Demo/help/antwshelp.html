How to connect to an ANT+ peripheral

  1. Insert the ANT+ Wahoo Key into the 30-pin connector of your iPhone to connect to ANT+ peripherals
  2. Activate your sensor and touch the connect button on the screen of your iPhone. Some weight scales might need a wake up command to start up. To send that push the "Send Profile" button first, to wake it up, and then the connect button to connect to it

Troubleshooting

The ANT+ Wahoo Key is inserted but not recognized!?!

This issue is almost always cause by dust, lint or other debris in the 30-pin connector. Syncing your iPhone only uses a few pins compared to the ANT+ Wahoo Key connector, so successful syncing and charging does not mean your connector is not contaminated. Cleaning the 30-pin connector with a cotton swab or canned air should fix this issue.
