Getting started

  1. Insert the ANT+ Wahoo Key into the 30-pin connector of your iPhone to connect to ANT+ peripherals
  2. Touch the icon of the required peripheral
    • HRM – connect to
      • heart rate monitor
    • Running – connect to
      • heart rate monitor and/or
      • stride sensor
    • Biking – connect to
      • heart rate monitor and/or
      • bike power sensor and/or
      • bike speed sensor and/or
      • bike cadence sensor or
      • bike speed/cadence combo sensor
    • Weight – connect to
      • weight scale/body composition analyzer
    • BP – connect to
      • blood pressure meter
    • File Transfer - connect to
      • Garmin sport watch
    • CGM - connect to
      • continues glucose meter
  3. Activate your sensor and touch the connect button on the screen of your iPhone

Configure settings

Open settings by touching the cog in the upper right corner of your iPhone

Troubleshooting

The ANT+ Wahoo Key is inserted but not recognized!?!

This issue is almost always cause by dust, lint or other debris in the 30-pin connector. Syncing your iPhone only uses a few pins compared to the ANT+ Wahoo Key connector, so successful syncing and charging does not mean your connector is not contaminated. Cleaning the 30-pin connector with a cotton swab or canned air should fix this issue.
